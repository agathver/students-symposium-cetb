Student’s Technical Symposium, CET, Bhubaneswar aims at providing an effective platform to the researchers from all over the state to showcase their original research work, have effective exchange of ideas and develop a strategic plan for balanced and inclusive growth of economy through IT in critical areas like E-Governance, E-Commerce, Disaster Management, GIS, Geospatial Technologies, Nanotechnology, Intellectual Property Rights, AI and Expert Systems, Networking, Software Engineering, High Performance Computing and other emerging technologies.

Student’s Technical Symposium, CET, Bhubaneswar will be held at College of Engineering and Technology, Bhubaneswar. The conference will provide a platform for technical exchanges within the research community and will encompass regular paper presentation sessions, invited talks, keynote addresses, panel discussions and poster exhibitions. In addition, the participants will be treated to a series of cultural activities, receptions and networking to establish new connections and foster everlasting friendship among fellow counterparts. The conference will also provide opportunity to the participants to enjoy the annual fest of CET, Bhubaneswar: Xtasy 2018.

Student’s Technical Symposium, CET, Bhubaneswar will be an amalgamation of four different Tracks organized parallel to each other, in addition to the 1st Ascend: Technical Exhibition and Knowledge Expo and few theme based Special Sessions, as listed below.

Full length original and unpublished papers, based on theoretical and experimental contributions, related, but not limited to, the following topics, are solicited for presentation and publication in the conference.
