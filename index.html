ISTE Technical Symposium, CET, Bhubaneswar aims at providing an effective platform to the researchers from all over the state to showcase their original research work, have effective exchange of ideas and develop a strategic plan for balanced and inclusive growth of economy through IT in critical areas like E-Governance, E-Commerce, Disaster Management, GIS, Geospatial Technologies, Nanotechnology, Intellectual Property Rights, AI and Expert Systems, Networking, Software Engineering, High Performance Computing and other emerging technologies.

ISTE Technical Symposium, CET, Bhubaneswar will be held at College of Engineering and Technology, Bhubaneswar. The conference will provide a platform for technical exchanges within the research community and will encompass regular paper presentation sessions, invited talks, keynote addresses, panel discussions and poster exhibitions. In addition, the participants will be treated to a series of cultural activities, receptions and networking to establish new connections and foster everlasting friendship among fellow counterparts. The conference will also provide opportunity to the participants to enjoy the annual fest of CET, Bhubaneswar: Xtasy 2018.

ISTE Technical Symposium, CET, Bhubaneswar will be an amalgamation of four different Tracks organized parallel to each other, in addition to the 1st Ascend: Technical Exhibition and Knowledge Expo and few theme based Special Sessions, as listed below.

Full length original and unpublished papers, based on theoretical and experimental contributions, related, but not limited to, the following topics, are solicited for presentation and publication in the conference.

Submition Guideline

ISTE Technical Symposium welcomes author for submission of papers related to Science, Engineering and Technology and their applications in business, industry and other relevant areas. The different innovative research is covered by this journal.

ISTE Technical Symposium is a monthly peer reviewed online journal.

The ISTE Technical Symposium invites paper contribution in the following categories:

  1. Original research
  2. Survey / Review articles, providing a comprehensive review on a scientific / research topic
  3. Extended version of conferences / Journals / Workshops
  4. Fast Communications: Short, self-contained articles on ongoing research
  5. Technical Notes

Guidelines to Contributors

Manuscripts are to be submitted to technicalsociety@cet.edu.in submit the manuscript as MS Word file along with the copy right form given below:

Link for paper format
  • All manuscripts must be the author(s) original, un-published work and must not be under - consideration for publication elsewhere. Each manuscript must include a 100 to 150 word abstract and a list of 5-to-7 keywords. The article length must be less than or equal to 8 pages.
  • Authors should submit proof-read and revised article. Before submission please makes sure that your paper is prepared using the ISTE Technical Symposium Single Column Paper Template. This will ensure the speedy process for publication.
  • Once the paper is accepted, the author(s) are assumed to send copyrights of the paper over to . If any copyrighted material is used by the author, the author must take permission for the same from the editor.
  • The submitted articles are reviewed by two independent reviewers. One of the reviewers will be a foreigner and another may be an Indian. Acceptance is granted when both the reviewers' recommendations are positive.
